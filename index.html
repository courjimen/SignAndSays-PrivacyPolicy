Privacy Policy for Sign & Says

Effective Date: May 6, 2026
Sign & Says is a communication tool designed to empower individuals who use signs, pictures, and speech tools. We prioritize user privacy and strive to collect only the minimum information necessary for the application to function.

Quick Summary

  • We do not track users or collect analytics
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties
  • We do not display advertisements
  • You maintain full control of all created content and data

Information Usage

You or a caregiver may create custom signs, picture cards, phrases, and care cards. This information is entered intentionally by the user and is stored locally to ensure the app functions as a personalized communication aid.

Text-to-Speech

The application provides text-to-speech functionality to assist in verbal communication. We do not record, store, or transmit any audio data generated through this feature.

Data We Do Not Collect

To ensure a private experience, Sign & Says does not access or collect any of the following:

  • Location data
  • Contact lists
  • Device photo libraries (unless manually selected for cards)
  • Messages or browsing history
  • Advertising identifiers

Children and Families

This app is designed for accessibility across all ages. Given its nature as a communication aid, it is intended for use by individuals and children with the support of caregivers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

You are in charge of your data. You can delete individual items directly within the app's interface. To remove all data associated with the application, you can simply delete the app from your device.
