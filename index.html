Privacy Policy for Sign & Says

Effective Date: 05/06/2026

Sign & Says is a communication app made to help people who use signs, pictures, and speech tools to communicate. We care deeply about privacy and try to collect as little information as possible.

Quick Summary

  • We do not track users
  • We do not sell or share personal data
  • We do not show ads
  • You stay in control of what you create and share

What Information Is Used

You or a caregiver may create signs, picture cards, phrases, and care cards. This information is entered on purpose and used only so the app works correctly for your specific needs.

Text-to-Speech

The app turns text into speech to facilitate communication. We do not record or save any audio generated through this feature.

What We Do Not Collect

To ensure your complete privacy, we do not access or store:

  • Location, contacts, or messaging history
  • Browsing history or advertising data
  • Photos (except for the ones you specifically choose to add to your cards)

Children and Families

The app is designed for children and adults with caregiver support. We follow strict safety standards to ensure a secure environment for all families.

Deleting Information

You are in total control. You can delete specific items within the app at any time, or remove all data entirely by deleting the app from your device.
